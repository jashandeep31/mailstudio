Generate a responsive MJML email template for a creative agency named "Alexa Studio," focusing on a modern, card-based design with a professional and vibrant aesthetic.

**Global Design Settings:**
- **Background Color:** Light light gray (#F3F5F8) for the body/outer wrapper.
- **Content Background:** White (#FFFFFF) for main content cards.
- **Primary Color:** Royal Blue (#3B82F6) for the header background, accent buttons, and links.
- **Secondary Color:** Black (#111827) for strong contrast sections.
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif font (Helvetica, Arial, or Roboto).
- **Styling:** Extensive use of rounded corners (border-radius: 12px to 16px) on images, buttons, and content blocks.

**Structure & Sections:**

1.  **Header:**
    - Full-width container with Primary Blue background.
    - White "Alexa Studio" logo or text aligned to the left.
    - Padding should be generous to create a header bar feel.

2.  **Hero Section:**
    - A white content wrapper with rounded corners containing:
    - A large, high-quality hero image showing a creative team working.
    - A small floating badge overlay on the image saying "2 slots available" (use a small pill-shaped container).
    - Headline text below image: "Welcome to Alexa Studio – Where Creativity Meets Innovation!"
    - Subtext: A personalized greeting ("Hi John,") followed by a brief introduction about helping brands stand out.

3.  **Services List ("What we offer"):**
    - A section with a light background (pale blue or gray).
    - Vertical list of services. Each service should be a row containing:
        - A small icon (left).
        - Service Name (e.g., "Branding & Identity", "Graphic Design", "Website & UI/UX").
        - A right-pointing arrow icon (right) to indicate it is clickable.
    - Separate items with subtle dividers or whitespace.

4.  **Dark Feature Card:**
    - A distinct section with a Black background and rounded corners.
    - Top: A row of pill-shaped tags (e.g., "AI", "Healthcare", "SaaS", "Web 3.0").
    - Headline: white text, "Start Your Creative Journey with Alexa Studio Today!"
    - CTA Button: Primary Blue background, "Book a Free Consultation".

5.  **Portfolio Highlight (Blue Section):**
    - Full-width or boxed section with Primary Blue background.
    - White text headline: "Your Creative Journey with Alexa Studio – How It Works!"
    - A central image area showing a collage/grid of portfolio work (mockups of apps, branding).
    - CTA Button: Black or Dark Blue, "Explore our portfolio".

6.  **Intro & Workflow Text:**
    - Clean white section.
    - Headline: "Hey, Just An Intro".
    - Body text explaining the smooth, structured workflow.
    - Primary Button: "Get in touch".

7.  **Promotional Offer:**
    - A card-style layout.
    - Headline: "Exclusive Offer! Get 20% Off Your First Creative Project".
    - Hero image of a sleek product or car (representing a client project).
    - Subtext emphasizing a limited-time offer.

8.  **Process Steps (Grid):**
    - A section titled "Limited-time offer. Don't miss out!"
    - A 2-column or 3-column responsive grid (stacking on mobile).
    - Each column contains a white card with:
        - A small user/action icon.
        - Text: "Book a free consultation", "Choose your creative service", and "Get 20% off – no hidden fees!".

9.  **Footer:**
    - Simple layout with the "Alexa Studio" logo.
    - Links for Unsubscribe and Privacy Policy.
    - Copyright text.

**Technical Constraints:**
- Ensure all images have `alt` text.
- Use `mj-button` for all CTAs with `border-radius` set to 25px (pill shape) or 8px (rounded rect).
- Ensure mobile responsiveness: multiple columns must stack vertically on mobile devices.
- Use proper padding (20px-40px) to prevent layout crowding.
